# Domain Instantiations
## Notes
- **STATUS**: Working draft, exploratory.
- **The four principle axes**:
    - **Agent Scope** (*Semantic Tiers*) — Adaptive | Agentic | Actuated | Self-actuated | Logogenic | Logozoetic
    - **Knowledge Type** *(activates at Agentic)* — Static | Learning
    - **Goal-Update Coupling** *(activates at Actuated)* — Separated | Coupled | Partial
    - **Arity** — Primitive | Composite
- **Notes column** carries what the bare lattice values don't already convey — any combination of:
    - the distinctive feature of this domain (what it does)
    - the AAD-machinery instantiation (how $M_t$, $\delta_t$, $\eta^*$, etc. map for this domain), where non-obvious or closed-form
    - pointers to relevant ASF segments via `#segment-slug` references
    - justification for the lattice placement when subtle
- *Italicized* domain names in `*( ... )*` mark domains that map onto the ontology but are *currently outside formal ASF scope* (biological, human-as-agent — ASF doesn't have segments formally treating these domains, even where they're mentioned as illustrative examples).
- ⭐ marker links to a worked example in the AAD appendices that directly instantiates the AAD machinery for that domain.

## What the mapping potentially reveals

### Tier clusters along physical / institutional / digital lines

Tiers 1-2 are dominated by *engineered / biological control systems* — thermostats, Kalman, PIDs, immune system, RL, MCTS — the probabilistic/Bayesian/variational family. Tier 3 is *human institutions and modular AI* — military units, DevOps teams, Scrum, hybrid robotics, developer-agents. Tier 4 is *biological creatures + goal-setting AI*. Tiers 5-6 are *language-constituted machines*. The lattice cleanly separates these clusters — useful sanity check that the ontology isn't collapsing distinctions the source domains preserve.

### Coupled is rare outside the language-constituted lineage

The Coupled column is sparse: active inference / hierarchical predictive processing at Actuated, intrinsic-motivation RL at Self-actuated (sometimes), and the Logogenic / Logozoetic LLM-substrate rows. Most biological and engineered systems achieve directed separation. AAD's claim that Coupled architectures are the *less common* case in nature holds up across the table.

### Scaffolding is a structural move

The scaffolded LLM agent flips both Coupling (Coupled → Partial) and Arity (Primitive → Composite) simultaneously. AI coding agents (Claude Code, Cursor, Copilot) instantiate this exactly: tool router + code-aware retrieval + test-runner re-introduces modularity the substrate lacks. Frontier-lab agentic-AI tooling is groping toward this structural move ad hoc; the ontology gives it precise vocabulary.

### Composition is common

*Composite* is at least as common as *Primitive* across Tiers 2-6: immune system, hybrid robotics, military unit, DevOps team, software-development project, eusocial colony, AutoGPT, scaffolded LLM, AI coding agent, deliberately-modular ELI. Section III's composition-closure machinery is therefore load-bearing for a substantial fraction of the framework's domain reach.
