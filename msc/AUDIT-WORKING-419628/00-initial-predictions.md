# Initial predictions — opus-r2c

## Topology of the framework as I understand it

ASF is a four-part research framework. Part I (AAD) is the mathematical core; the other three are domain instantiations (TST = software; Logogenic = language-channel agents; Logozoetic = morally-weighted persistence). The integration story is genuinely the contribution — these are mature disciplines (control theory, causal inference, information theory, agent architecture) being threaded under one formalism for adaptive, purposeful agents.

The load-bearing architecture in AAD has three axes:

1. **The adaptive cycle** — Prolepsis / Aisthesis / Aporia / Epistrophe / Praxis. One traversal of the loop. Greek vocabulary is deliberate; each name picks out a distinction English flattens.
2. **Three-section progression** — Section I (adaptive systems, Lyapunov-grounded persistence machinery), Section II (actuated agents with $G_t = (O_t, \Sigma_t)$ goal/strategy split, directed-separation classification), Section III (composition, adversarial dynamics). Maturity decreases left to right.
3. **Three meta-segments** — `disc-separability-pattern`, `disc-identifiability-floor`, `disc-additive-coordinate-forcing`. These are AAD's cross-sectional lenses; each segment can be read through them to surface what's load-bearing.

The persistence condition $\alpha > \rho/R$ is the most-instantiated result. Satisfaction-gap / control-regret split is the diagnostic-core contribution. Loop-as-Level-2-causal-engine is the structural novelty.

## What I expect to be most novel

The integration is the contribution — nothing structurally novel about Lyapunov stability or Pearl's hierarchy alone. What's novel is the *combination*:

- **Persistence condition as cross-domain inequality** — same $\alpha > \rho/R$ instantiates as Kalman stability margin / RL convergence / org viability / SW maintainability. The dimensional consistency is the substance.
- **Loop as Level-2 causal engine** — the agent-environment feedback loop *itself* supplies interventional access. Purely observational learners don't have this.
- **Directed separation as architectural classification** — not a parametric continuum but a discrete partition (Class 1 / 2 / 3) with explicit Class 2 scope exit.
- **Satisfaction gap / control regret split** — orthogonal diagnostics that route to different interventions. Crispest named pair in the project.
- **Calibration-laboratory framing for software** — not "software is the best operationalization domain" but "software is where AAD's machinery can be most cleanly grounded; other domains inherit under explicit transfer assumptions." The transfer-assumption discipline prevents accidental overclaim.
