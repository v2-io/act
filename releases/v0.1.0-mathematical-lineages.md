### Lineages prominent but missing from README's "Position & Lineage" headline paragraph

- **Information geometry / Fisher-Rao / Čencov** (~23 segments). Fisher metric is *forced* coordinate-by-coordinate via Čencov 1982 in the additive-coordinate-forcing meta-pattern, AAD-internally. This is one of AAD's most distinctive moves and is invisible in the README headline.
- **Game theory** (~20 segments, mostly Section III). Adversarial dynamics, partially-opposing objectives, potential games (Monderer-Shapley), monotone games (Rosen) — none of this appears in the README's lineage paragraph. Section III's adversarial-tempo headline result *is* a game-theoretic result.
- **Optimization theory / LMI / Lagrangian** (~18 segments). LMI on FIM (Boyd-style PSD multipliers), IB Lagrangian, MDL. Connective tissue spanning IB, strategy-cost, and the causal-IB exploration drive.
- **Dynamical systems** (~20 segments). ODE / transient amplification / non-normal operators (in 03-logogenic) / singular perturbation. Currently subsumed under "control theory" in the README, but the framework leans on dynamical-systems machinery distinct from feedback-control machinery.
- **Reinforcement learning / Bellman** (~17 segments). The convention-hierarchy C1/C2/C3 monotonicity is Bellman-grounded; example-bandit is a full RL worked example.

### Lineages where coverage is thin relative to framing weight

- **Statistical learning theory** (~3 segments). Model-class capacity is named but no PAC-style or sample-complexity machinery is developed.
- **Active inference / Friston** (~8 segments, mostly cross-references). The framework deliberately positions itself adjacent-but-not-identical to active inference. If this positioning matters externally, it could carry more weight.
- **Thermodynamics** (~6 segments). The Maxwellian-demon / Landauer-analog framing in `deriv-persistence-cost` and its echo in `hyp-the-three-deaths` is striking but thinner in segment count than in conceptual weight.

### Other observations

- **The "AAD-internal forcing" pattern.** Five segments (`disc-additive-coordinate-forcing`, `der-chain-confidence-decay`, `deriv-edge-update-natural-parameter`, `deriv-fisher-whitened-update-rule`, `deriv-strategy-cost-regret-bound`) carry a distinctive structure: an AAD-internally-motivated axiom + an external uniqueness theorem (Cauchy-FE / Čencov / Cox) → a forced coordinate. This is arguably AAD's most distinctive *methodological* signature.
- **TST is unusually narrow.** TST (25 segments) draws almost exclusively from causal inference, information theory, Bayesian, and RL. It instantiates AAD's machinery in a domain rather than developing further lineages — consistent with TST's calibration-laboratory framing.
- **Logogenic (03) inherits, doesn't add new lineages.** All 15 segments draw from already-established lineages; the distinctive content is *architectural* (channel collapse, scaffolded recovery, interiority loop) rather than mathematical.
- **04-eli's developmental-psychology lineage is real and substantively used**, not decorative. Recommend explicit credit in v0.1.0+ release framing.
